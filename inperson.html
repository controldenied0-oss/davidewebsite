In Person Drum Lessons

Professional one-to-one drum lessons in a focused, supportive, and musical learning environment.

In-person drum lessons give students the most direct and natural way to learn. Being in the same room makes it easier to demonstrate technique clearly, correct posture and movement, improve timing, and respond immediately to what the student needs in the moment.

Lessons are tailored around the individual. Some students want to start from the very beginning and build confidence behind the kit, while others want a more structured path covering reading, coordination, groove, technique, and exam preparation.

The aim is to make every lesson enjoyable, focused, and productive. Students progress at the right pace while building the musical foundations that help them become confident and expressive drummers.

  • One-to-one personalised drum lessons
  • Lessons for children, teenagers, and adults
  • Beginner to advanced levels welcome
  • Preparation for Trinity and Rockschool exams

What You Will Learn

★

Students work on a mixture of practical and musical skills that build strong overall drumming ability. Lessons can include timing exercises, coordination development, technique work, notation reading, grooves, fills, and learning songs across different styles.

For students who want a more structured route, lessons can also be geared towards Trinity or Rockschool graded drum exams, helping them prepare properly while continuing to grow as complete musicians.
